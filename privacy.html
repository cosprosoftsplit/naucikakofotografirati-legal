Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Nauči Kako Fotografirati ("the App") is developed by SoftSplit d.o.o., Split, Croatia. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are included.
  • No personal identifiers are collected.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

Photos & Camera

If the App accesses your photo library or camera, all images remain stored locally on your device. Photos are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Lesson Progress & Streaks

Your lesson progress and streak data are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external server.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit framework. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
