Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nauči Kako Fotografirati?

Nauči Kako Fotografirati is a photography learning app that teaches you how to take better photos through 48 structured daily lessons. Each lesson covers a different aspect of photography — from composition and lighting to advanced techniques. The app is available in 20 languages.

How does the streak system work?

Each day you complete a lesson, your streak counter increases by one. Maintaining a daily streak helps build a consistent learning habit. If you miss a day, your streak resets. Open the app daily and complete your lesson to keep your streak alive.

What's included in Premium?

Premium unlocks the full set of 48 photography lessons. Free users can access a selection of introductory lessons. A one-time purchase gives you lifetime access to all current and future content with no subscriptions or recurring charges.

How do I change the language?

The app follows your device's language setting. To change the language, go to your device's Settings → General → Language & Region and select your preferred language. The app supports 20 languages including English, Croatian, German, Spanish, French, Italian, and more.

How do I restore purchases?

If you have previously purchased Premium and need to restore it (for example, after reinstalling the app or switching devices), open the app and tap "Restore Purchases" on the premium screen. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
